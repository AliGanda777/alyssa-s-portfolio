GOJO SATORU

"Throughout Heaven and Earth, I alone am the Honored One."

GOJO

Gojo Satoru is known as the strongest jujutsu sorcerer, a figure who towers above all others in his world.

His declaration, "Throughout heaven and earth, I alone am the honored one," reflects both his unmatched confidence and the reality of his power.

With the rare combination of the Six Eyes and the Limitless technique, he bends space itself, making him untouchable and nearly invincible.
