THE STRONGEST

Satoru Gojo, Special Grade Sorcerer

The One Who Stands Above All

Born as the first sorcerer in 400 years to inherit both the Six Eyes and the Limitless technique, Satoru Gojo disrupted the balance of the jujutsu world simply by existing. As a teacher at Tokyo Jujutsu High, he mentors the next generation while reshaping the corrupt jujutsu society from within.

Gojo's birth alone shifted the balance of power in the jujutsu world. His presence weakened cursed spirits and forced even the most ancient entities to reconsider their strategies. The combination of the Six Eyes and the Limitless, two abilities that had not coexisted in a single sorcerer for four centuries, made him a living anomaly.

Beyond his overwhelming strength, Gojo is a reformist. He recognized early on that the jujutsu world's rigid hierarchy and outdated traditions held back talented sorcerers while protecting those in power. By becoming a teacher at Tokyo Jujutsu High, he chose to invest in the next generation, nurturing students like Yuji Itadori, Megumi Fushiguro, and Nobara Kugisaki into capable sorcerers who could one day stand on their own.

His philosophy is simple: true strength is not about standing alone at the top, but about raising others to stand beside you. Every lesson, every fight, and every sacrifice Gojo makes is in service of a future where the jujutsu world is defined by merit, not by bloodline or politics.
