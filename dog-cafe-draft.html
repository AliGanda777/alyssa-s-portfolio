Cafe Visit Guide

A quiet afternoon at Paws & Pour, where Oakleys, Aurora, and Naruto keep the coffee company.

April 18, 2026 · 5 min read · Back to Portfolio

Paws & Pour is a small neighborhood cafe built around three very different dogs. It's the kind of place where the espresso machine hisses in the background, a tail thumps under the next table, and a slow afternoon feels earned. Here are the three residents who keep it running.

No. 01

Oakleys

Greeter & Ambassador

Oakleys is the first face you meet at the door. He has a warm brown coat, a soft bark, and the kind of steady temperament that puts first-time visitors at ease. Whether you are a regular or a walk-in, expect an unhurried hello and a tail wag you can set your watch to.

Warm-hearted Social People-oriented

He has a quiet talent for reading the room. A cuddle when you want one, space when you don't, and good company either way. If you are here for photos, he is patient and photogenic, which helps.

No. 02

Aurora

The Adventurous Playmate

Aurora is the cafe's burst of motion. Lean, bright-eyed, and happiest when a toy is involved, she brings a cheerful pace to the room without tipping into chaos. She is the older sister to Naruto by one year, and the reason the staff keeps a second rope toy behind the counter.

Energetic Joyful Adventurous

She is ideal for younger guests and anyone who prefers active company. Expect short bursts of play, a nudge toward the nearest toy, and a quick trot back to her mat when it's time to rest. She knows when to pause.

No. 03

Naruto

The Quiet One

Where Oakleys greets, Naruto observes. He is the quieter half of the house, carrying himself with an ease that suits slower afternoons. The younger brother to Aurora by a year, he is most often found on the bench near the window, letting the cafe come to him.

Calm Elegant Thoughtful

His best trait is attentiveness. He tends to notice when a guest needs company and will quietly lean in without making a production of it. If you come in looking for a calm hour, Naruto is the one.

How to Visit

Location & Hours

Open daily from 10:00 to 18:00. Walk-ins welcome; reservations recommended on weekends.

Best Time to Visit

Weekday afternoons between 14:00 and 16:00 tend to be quietest. Weekends are livelier and better suited to groups.

What to Expect

An unhurried welcome from Oakleys, Aurora, and Naruto. Comfortable seating, a short coffee and tea menu, and room to move between tables at your own pace.

Photography

Natural light near the window is best in the late afternoon. Flash is discouraged out of respect for the dogs.

Guest Notes

Treats are provided; please ask before offering anything of your own. Dress comfortably. A small amount of fur is part of the experience.
